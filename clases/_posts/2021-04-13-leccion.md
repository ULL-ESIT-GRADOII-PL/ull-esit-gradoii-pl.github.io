Most modern browsers, however, do offer property available called <code><a href="https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/JavaScript/Reference/Global_Objects/Object/proto">__proto__</a></code> (that's 2 underscores either side), which contains the object's constructor's <code>prototype</code> object. For example, try <code>person1.__proto__</code> and <code>person1.__proto__.__proto__</code> to see what the chain looks like in code!
